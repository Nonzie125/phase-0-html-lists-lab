Ingredients for making Grilled cheese

  • 2 slices of bread

  • 4 slices of cheese
  1. mozarella
  2. pepper jack
  3. Cheddar
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
    1. Spread butter on bread and a frying pan
    2. Place bread on a fruing pan and fry
    3. add cheese on top of the frying bread
    4. cover with second slice of bread
    5. Turnover and fry for two minutes
